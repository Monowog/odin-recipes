Broccoli Stir-Fry

Description

This dish has been a staple of my diet for years. It has everything you could ask for in a meal, powerful flavors, plenty of spice, a cheap cost to make, and myriad health benefits from all the cruciferous greens. Please enjoy my spin on it... Hopefully you like it hot!

Ingredients

  • Broccoli crowns and stems, a few cups
  • Cooking oil of choice, a couple teaspoons
  • Garlic, 4-6 cloves
  • Onion, 1/2 chopped
  • Jalapeno or Serrano Peppers, 1/2 cup
  • Cholula hot sauce, to taste
  • Extra Firm Tofu, 400 grams diced
  • Paprika, 2 teaspoons
  • White or Brown Rice
  • Low Sodium Soy Sauce, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the broccoli into bite-sized pieces, and dice the onion, garlic, and pepper.
  2. Heat your choice of oil in a nonstick pan on medium heat.
  3. Toss the garlic and onion into the hot pan, and stir until onions turn translucent.
  4. Place the broccoli and peppers into the pan, along with a splash of water to steam.
  5. Cover the pan and cook for roughly three minutes undisturbed.
  6. Remove the lid, then pour in a few good dashes of soy sauce, along with your paprika.
  7. Add the diced tofu into the pan as well. Allow to rest until tofu is cooked.
  8. Serve on top of fluffy rice with a healthy amount of Cholula, and enjoy!
